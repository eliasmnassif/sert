About SERT

"Société d'Etudes et de Réalisation Techniques" (SERT) brings decades of expertise to every project. Our engineers are adept at addressing complex issues across a wide spectrum of systems.

Our Expertise Includes:

  • HVAC systems: central energy plants, packaged air/water cooled systems, split/unitary systems.
  • Specialized systems: smoke control, energy conservation, humidity control, indoor air quality, laminar flow.
  • Plumbing: sanitary, domestic water, booster pumps, central hot water, water treatment, solar heating, fueling systems.
  • Electrical systems: illumination design, safety and code compliance, power generation, transmission, and distribution (low, medium, high voltage).
  • Safety: fire alarm, security, voice notification, emergency power and lighting.

We pride ourselves on a meticulous approach, where every design is checked and double-checked to eradicate weaknesses and ensure optimal performance and safety. SERT is uniquely qualified to partner with you in developing master plans, preparing feasibility studies, and designing the mechanical and electrical systems for new facilities or renovations.
